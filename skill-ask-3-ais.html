Ask 3 AIs at Once

One prompt → three answers as colored cards stacked under each other → one final card where Claude keeps what's genuinely good from all three. A super-prompt that fans a single question out to ChatGPT, Perplexity and Claude in parallel, shows each one's output in its own colored card, then combines the best of the lot into one.

Why use it

Every AI has its own bias and blind spots. ChatGPT is conversational and broad. Perplexity is source-heavy and current. Claude is structured and nuanced. Asking only one means you only get one perspective. This skill fires the exact same prompt to all three at once, shows you their raw answers side-by-side, then synthesizes the strongest points into a single merged "best of" answer.

How to use it

STEP 1 — INSTALL THE SKILL

Click Download Skill above. You get an ask-3-ais-at-once.md file. In Claude, open Settings → Capabilities → Skills and upload the file. The skill is now available across all your conversations.

STEP 2 — CONNECT BRIGHT DATA MCP

The skill uses Bright Data to fetch what ChatGPT and Perplexity actually say. In Claude, go to Settings → Connectors → Add custom connector and paste this URL (replace YOUR_API_TOKEN with your Bright Data token):

https://mcp.brightdata.com/sse?token=YOUR_API_TOKEN&groups=ai_insights

One-time setup. Get your Bright Data token at brightdata.com/cp/setting/users.

STEP 3 — TRIGGER THE SKILL

In any conversation, just say it naturally:

  • "Ask all 3 AIs: what's the best CRM for a 5-person sales team?"
  • "Send this to ChatGPT, Perplexity and Claude — what are the top SEO trends in 2026?"
  • "Compare LLM answers on this: should I use Make or n8n?"
  • "What do the 3 AIs say about Bright Data?"

You can also just say "use Ask 3 AIs" and Claude will ask you for the prompt to run.

STEP 4 — READ THE 3 ANSWERS, THEN THE BEST OF

Claude shows you a colored visual right in the chat:

  • Teal card → what ChatGPT said
  • Purple card → what Perplexity said
  • Coral card → what Claude said (web search)
  • Blue card → "Best of the three" — Claude takes the strongest points from each, drops the weak ones, and gives you one clean answer

The whole thing is screenshot-ready and easy to share with your team.

When it's most useful

  • Buying decisions — "best CRM / SEO tool / project management app for [my use case]" → see if all 3 AIs converge on a name or if they disagree.
  • Strategic questions — "should I do X or Y?" → get three independent perspectives before deciding.
  • Brand visibility — "what do you know about [my company]?" → see how each AI describes you and what they get wrong.
  • Fact-checking — if all 3 AIs agree on a fact, it's probably right. If they disagree, you need to dig deeper.
  • Trend research — "what's hot in [industry] right now?" → catch what one AI misses.

Good to know

  • The prompt is sent exactly as you wrote it to all three — no rewording per engine, so the comparison is fair.
  • If one AI fails to respond (rare), it's marked as skipped and the merge happens on the other two.
  • Claude's answer is its own web-search response — not a scrape of the Claude app.
  • The most interesting cases are when the three disagree — the merged "Best of" tells you who said what and why it kept what it kept.
